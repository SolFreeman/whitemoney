Take 5 steps to your own love story!

In order for you to get all the benefits from working with a matchmaker, enjoy the process and get the best result we offer:

Questionnaire

You will be proposed to FILL IN THE FORM. This procedure allows for more accurate and responsive partner search, as well as greater flexibility in adjusting the parameters. The form reveals substantive information about your personality, hobbies and pastime, educational and family background, your most valuable attributes, view of ideal partner and married life in general. Your personal English-speaking assistant would reply to any of the questions you possibly have. Afterwards the first FREE interview with Elena Kovalevskaya personally will be scheduled.

FIRST FREE INTERVIEW

During the personal interview Elena Kovalevskaya will enter into your feelings, needs, values and expectations. We must ensure that your views are not contrary to accepted moral standards, principles of Happy Family agency or general matchmaking ethics. As a result of the interview Elena would assume whether the client has serious intentions and whether the required number of women candidates are there in our base and in the base of the reliable matchmaking platform. Then you will be suggested to sign the contract and consult other important documents.

VIDEO DATES with potential ladies

Having worked with our women base and the base of the matchmaking platform, we propose 25-30 profiles, selected specifically on your parameters. After viewing the girls selected, you choose those who you liked the most. Your personal assistant will give you special advices on every woman and arrange video dates. Please notice that during the first video conversations, contact exchange is forbidden. In the event that your expectations and needs are even more sophisticated, we would hire the services of our recruiters, who conduct additional searches. After each video conversation, we receive feedback from you and the woman, and only if the interest is mutual on both sides, we mark this lady as a priority for a personal meeting with you. When you have the required number of mutually interested ladies, according to the package of services, we can plan your tour to Ukraine and individual meetings with women.

Offline Dating

We will help you to spend the meeting in a secure, pleasant and stress-free environment. The arrangement of the meeting through our agency is a guarantee that the lady shows up. Happy Family agency is a company with a closed-loop model: our stuff can provide all kind of services our clients need at any step (except sex tours and other prohibited services), so nothing would distract you from dating. Since we are professionals you can get extra services at each stage: we can help you to find an apartment or book the hotel room, we can meet you at the airport and make your stay comfortable and safe, we can arrange flower delivery, excursions, classical and unusual dates, that both will remember forever, etc.

DEVELOPMENT OF RELATIONSHIP

Having met the chosen women in Ukraine, you would already know which to continue communication with. Up to this step we hope you’d feel mutual attraction and interest with one or several ladies, gradually you establish an entirely new level of confidence in relations with ladies. Happy Family Matchmaking agency and Elena Kovalevskaya personally will keep consulting you and your chosen woman at this stage, because her matchmaking coaching and professional experience can help you overcome a paper-wall, possible mentality and cultural differences or other misunderstandings. This stage is important for us, because a huge amount of work would be implemented to help you reach this stage.
